Gopal Maske

Founder and Chief Executive Officer

Gopal Maske has more than 40 years of experience in designing and building complex industrial fabrication modules. Ever Since, Excellent Engineering has expanded its wings across North America, South America, Europe Asia, Middle East and Africa.
